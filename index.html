The problem

The iteration loop is where hardware goes to die.

Software engineers ship a change and see it in seconds. Hardware engineers ship a board to a fab and see it next week — then find the one mistake and start the clock over. Every revision is a multi-day round trip. PrometheusPCB collapses that loop to the length of a coffee break, so the bottleneck stops being logistics and goes back to being your ideas.
